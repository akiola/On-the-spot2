Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a transfer take?

Most transfers are completed within minutes. However, depending on the destination and payment method, some transfers may take up to 24 hours to process.

What payment methods can I use?

We accept payments through MTN Mobile Money, Telecel Money, AT Money, and Visa/Mastercard credit or debit cards.

Is there a minimum or maximum transfer amount?

The minimum transfer amount is 10 GHS. The maximum transfer amount varies by destination but is typically up to 50,000 GHS per transaction for verified accounts.

How do I track my transfer?

You can track your transfer using the transaction ID provided at the time of payment. Simply enter this ID on our website or contact our customer support for updates.

What if my recipient doesn't receive the money?

In the rare event that a transfer is not received, please contact our customer support with your transaction ID. We will investigate the issue and resolve it promptly.

Can I cancel a transfer?

Transfers can only be canceled if they have not yet been claimed by the recipient. Contact our customer support immediately if you need to cancel a transfer.
