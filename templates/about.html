Our Story

Founded in 2020, On the Spot Transfer was born out of a simple vision: to make international money transfers from Ghana fast, secure, and accessible to everyone. Our founder, after experiencing the frustration of slow and expensive traditional money transfer services, set out to create a better solution.

What started as a small operation serving the Ghana-Nigeria corridor has now expanded to include transfers to the United States, France, and Canada, with plans to add more countries in the near future.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers across Ghana, helping them send money to their loved ones abroad quickly and affordably.

Our Mission

To provide fast, secure, and affordable money transfer services that connect Ghanaians to their loved ones around the world, empowering financial inclusion and cross-border economic activity.

Our Vision

To be the leading money transfer service in West Africa, known for reliability, innovation, and customer-centric solutions that bridge financial gaps across borders.
